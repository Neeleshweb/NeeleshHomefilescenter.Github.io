About me

It was while observing a friend play ‘Battleship’, a game where battleships would be hidden in square coordinates that I first identified my own skill in implementing Machine Learning. Here, I noticed the trend that players firing even numbers was more likely to sink ships in minimum attempts. Thus began a fascination with Machine Learning prior to my undergraduate degree which propelled my interest in taking up Baccalaureate studies in Information Technology. Having developed a deep passion for AI, I harbor ambitions of becoming an innovator in the AI industry and turning entrepreneur in the long run.

My undergraduate program was well-structured and helped me develop a broad understanding of different areas. Over time, I gained a vivid interest in implementing automation and ML in web development and employed my ideas in the design of a HomeFilesCenter website, that helped the customers of all over the world to share their reviews of a product after using it and then based on their reviews, the companies that manufactured that product was ranked on the dynamic ranking portal. Moreover, the success rate of the product in the future market was predicted by various predictive algorithms that I created for the result.

Soon , i understood that a website without an android app is not a good idea, so i developed an android application for the website as well. On graduating, I wished to continue learning about the emerging trends in Computer Science and therefore, joined TCS and began working on Enterprise Resource Management systems.
